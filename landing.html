How it works
From ticket to
traceable outcome
Each step is designed so automation accelerates the queue while accountability stays with your team.

1. Ingest & classify

Duplicates, shipping failures, damaged goods, and refund asks are separated from generic “help” noise—so the model works on the right problem class, not one-size replies.

2. Propose within guardrails

Xalvion surfaces a concrete next action: message, credit, refund, escalate, or hold. Amounts respect caps; sensitive paths stay flagged; nothing customer-facing ships without the approval model you configure.

3. Approve, execute, record

Your operator confirms or edits once. The workspace captures what was decided, why, and who signed off—so leadership, QA, and finance see the same operational truth.
